SAUSAGE MAN

Sausage Man involves 100 players in the form of sausage humans who are in a certain area. Game Play Sausage Man, all players will be taken by an airplane and players will do a free fall to land themselves to the battle ground. After the player lands, the player will look for weapons, ammunition, medical equipment, bullet-proof vests, bags, batons, excalibur swords and character identity cards that can give players special effects such as shadow warriors and sweety. In the game Sausage Man, there are vehicles to travel from cars that become robots, UFOs, cars to even dragons. Players will continue to move and fight with each other and walk to stay in the safe zone. If a player is outside the safe zone, over time the player will be automatically eliminated. Players can choose to play solo, duet and quad with 4 players. Players can choose a play style such as no tactics, look for weapons and play it safe. In the game there are three modes, namely classic party, arcade party and training party. The currency used to buy items such as outfits, weapons, vehicles and so on is candy. You can get Candy or seasonal pass medals for free on the menu every day. Items that you buy using candy are determined the results will get through a random draw. So the luck factor is the main thing, even if you have a lot of money to buy candy.
