PILOT

INTRODUCTION

Pilot is a piloting service that can boost the account ranking of applicants that apply to us. We can provide the applicant with a different specialized game. We can provide a multi-games service that will improve the account or rank of the applicant who submits an application to us. Mobile Legends: Bang Bang, CODM (Call of Duty Mobile), Pokemon Unite, PUBG (PlayerUnknown's BattleGrounds Mobile), and Sausage Man are the only mobile games offered on our website. The applicant will benefit from the piloting service if he does not have time to play the game. We will play his/her account and, in addition to playing their account, we will raise their account to whatever level of rank he wants.

OBJECTIVES

The main goals of our website are to offer applicants who apply to us to play and improve their rank, as well as to offer them the opportunity to have their account pushed to the top of the game if they so want.

SCOPE AND LIMITATIONS

The scope of our website is that the user can apply to us for free, and we also have five different games that we can offer to the applicant who applies to us. They can choose any of these games, and we can guarantee that their account will be safe. We can also boost their levels or ranking, and we can also guarantee that we will get their account to the top of the game if they want.

The following are the limitations of our website: They may not trust us since if they apply to us, they must first pay us in order for us to play their account. There are only five different games that we provide. We are unable to specify a definite date when their account will be upgraded to the desired rating.
